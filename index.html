About me

I am a Postdoctoral Associate in the Department of Biostatistics at Yale University under the supervision of Prof. Heping Zhang. My current research interests focus on variable selection, high-dimensional statistics, empirical Bayes and imaging-genetics. Before coming to Yale, I obtained my Ph.D in Statistics from Rutgers University, and advised by Profs. Cun-Hui Zhang and Lee Dicker.
