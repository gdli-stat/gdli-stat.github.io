About me

I am an Assistant Professor in the School of Data Science, City University of Hong Kong. Before joining CityU, I was a Postdoctoral Associate in the Department of Biostatistics at Yale University, supervised by Prof. Heping Zhang. My current research interests focus on image data analysis, convolutional neural networks, high-dimensional statistics, machine learning, etc. I obtained my Ph.D in Statistics from Rutgers University, advised by Profs. Cun-Hui Zhang and Lee Dicker.
