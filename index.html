About me

I am an Assistant Professor in the School of Data Science, City University of Hong Kong. Before joining CityU, I was a Postdoctoral Associate in the Department of Biostatistics at Yale University. My current research interests focus on imaging data analysis, including image regression, image clustering, image denoising, region detection in image data, etc. I obtained my Ph.D in Statistics from Rutgers University.
