Biography

I joined the Department of Statistics & Actuarial Science, University of Hong Kong, in 2009 as an Assistant Professor, and I am currently a Professor. Prior to this, I had worked at the Division of Mathematical Sciences, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, as an Assistant Professor since I received my PhD degree in statistics from the University of Hong Kong in 2007. I got my Bachelor and Master degrees in Statistics from Peking University.

Research

My research interests include

  • Time series analysis

  • Quantile regression

  • High-dimensional data
