About me

I am an Assistant Professor in the School of Data Science, City University of Hong Kong. Before joining CityU, I was a Postdoctoral Associate in the Department of Biostatistics at Yale University under the supervision of Prof. Heping Zhang. My current research interests focus on high-dimensional statistics, imaging data analysis, and empirical Bayes methods. I obtained my Ph.D in Statistics from Rutgers University, and advised by Prof. Cun-Hui Zhang and Prof. Lee Dicker.

Education

  • 2017, Ph.D. in Statistics, Rutgers University

  • 2012, B.S. in Statistics and Mathematics, Renmin University of China

Research

My research interests include
